The Problem & Solution
💸
Problem
Subscriptions are invisible money leaks

With the rise of subscription services — Netflix, gyms, apps, SaaS — people lose track of recurring payments, often paying for services they no longer use. Banking apps hide these in a long list of transactions.

🔍
Solution
A dedicated subscription intelligence layer that sits on top of banking

FinGuard scans your transactions, automatically detects recurring payments, categorises them by usage likelihood, and alerts you before renewals — so you're always in control of what you're paying for.

How It Works
STEP 01
🔗
Connect your bank (read-only)

Link via Account Aggregator (AA) framework — read-only, RBI-compliant. No credentials shared.

STEP 02
🤖
AI detects recurring payments

ML model identifies recurring charges from merchant patterns, frequency, and amount — even when merchant names are truncated.

STEP 03
⚠️
Flags unused or risky subs

Cross-references app usage data (with permission) to flag "possibly unused" subscriptions. Highlights free trials expiring soon.

STEP 04
🔔
Pre-renewal alerts

Get notified 3 days before any subscription renews. One tap to cancel directly or set a reminder.

Core Features
🔄
Auto-detection

Identifies recurring payments across all linked accounts — even those with irregular billing dates.

🧠
Usage Intelligence

Optionally integrates with phone screen time to flag apps you haven't opened in 30+ days but are still paying for.

📅
Renewal Calendar

See every upcoming renewal in a calendar view. Never be surprised by a ₹2,000 annual subscription again.

🛑
One-tap Cancel Links

Direct links to subscription cancellation pages — no hunting through settings on each platform.
